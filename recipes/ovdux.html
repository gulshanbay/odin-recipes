Owdukh

Description

This is a traditional cold drink or soup from Azerbaijan. It is refreshing and super healthy.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of plain yougurt or kefir
  • 1 medium size cucumber
  • Handful of chopped cilantro, dill, fresh green onions and basil
  • Salt
  • Water

Preparation

Whisk the yogurt and add cold water until the desired consitency. Add cucumber chopped into small pieces. Then add all greens and salt to your taste. Mix and you can drink it or add some croutons and eat it as a soup.

Bon Apetit!
