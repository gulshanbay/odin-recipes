Pilaff

Description

This receipe is about how to prepare the most basic pilaff from Azerbaijan. By far this is the healthiest way of preparing rice for it will lose most of chemicals and starch it contains by the time it is ready to eat.You can add various precooked beansor dry fruits to your pilaff and call them by that name. Also it is videly used method to prepare a special kind of crusty and delicious cake for the bedding of rice to prevent it from burning and extra enjoyment.(Refer to the photo above)

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of rice (Basmati rice for the best result)
  • 2 tbs salt
  • 200g butter>
  • 1.5l water

Preparation

  1. Wash the rice
  2. Cut the butter into several pieces

Cooking

Boil the water and add salt when boiling. Taste and make sure it is a bit saltier than normal taste. Wash the rice until clean water runs through it and then add to the boiling water. mix and let it cook. When the rice is cooked, take it and pour it into the sieve. Put the clean casserole back onto the heat add a tablespoon of butter. Butter is melted and hot add the rice back. cut the rest of butter into pieces and add on top of rice. cover the casserole with towel and put the lid on. Vaporized water should not go back to the rice. after about half an hour your pilaff is ready.

It is commonly eaten with a side dish containig either meat, chicken, dried fruits or herbs, There are countless types of pilaffs and it may vary depending on the imaagination and courage of the cook to try new things.

Bon Apetit!
